# Open Source Society: By Humankind, For Humankind

*(working title — not yet finalized)*

This book exists to make good on a promise from the original [Open Source Society GoFundMe](https://www.gofundme.com/f/open-source-society): a blueprint for a coordinated, equitable path forward for humanity — for anyone who wants to act on the brokenness of the world, regardless of faith.

Its companion, [*Christianity Reconstructed in 24 Hours*](https://github.com/trusthumankind/christianity-in-24-hours), makes the same underlying case through a Christian lens, ending with an on-ramp to this mission. This book is the other door — no theology required, straight to the problems and the roadmap.

## The five victory conditions

Both books converge on the same five systemic goals. This book's job is to make the case for each with urgency, then assemble what already exists — research, precedent, proposed models — into one unified blueprint no single existing work has combined:

1. **One country** — no borders, free movement of all people
2. **One currency** — no financial arbitrage between nations
3. **Food security** for every human being
4. **Housing security** for every human being
5. **Education without gatekeepers**

See [OUTLINE.md](OUTLINE.md) for what's already known about each, and what a unifying blueprint still has to originate.

## How this book is written

Two different jobs, not one:

- **Urgency sections** — light curation, not original investigation. Retell the most viscerally compelling *existing* human stories and cases. Citations alone don't create urgency; narrative does.
- **Blueprint sections** — heavy synthesis, not invention. Don't originate new economic or political mechanisms — assemble serious existing research (open-borders economics, monetary union precedent, Housing First, education-equity research) into one coherent roadmap.
